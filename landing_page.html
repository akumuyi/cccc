The African Leadership University | Alumni Nexus

... a global network of ethical change makers!

The ALU Alumni Nexus is the dynamic alumni
community of the African Leadership University.
Our primary aim is to provide a digital platform
that enables ALU alums to stay connected and
informed about upcoming events and activities,
fostering a sense of continued camaraderie,
networking, and lifelong learning.

Our Agenda

Provide a central hub for ALU alumni to access details about upcoming events, including
but not limited to workshops, reunions, networking sessions, and social gatherings.
Strengthen connections among alumni by facilitating communication and engagement.
Professional Development: Offer opportunities for alumni to enhance their professional
skills and network with fellow alumni and industry experts.
Showcase success stories and educational content to inspire and empower alumni in their
career journeys.
Foster a sense of belonging and pride within the ALU alumni community by providing a
space for alumni to share their experiences, achievements, and ideas.

About ALU

Alu provides higher education for a higher purpose. Our students
declare missions, not majors.

They develop the real-world skills to take on the world's most
pressing challenges. And they take ownership of their learning from
day one through our peer and student-led-approach - because ALU
believes in the power and agency of young people to start shaping
the future right now.

Together with a world-class faculty and staff, our students are igniting
a ripple of positive impact across Africa and the world.
